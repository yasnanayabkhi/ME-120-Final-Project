# PDB Procedure

Website to obtain 1C9 (Dacomitinib): https://www.rcsb.org/ligand/1C9
Website to obtain 4DXD (FtsZ with GDP/723): https://www.rcsb.org/structure/4DXD

Procedure to convert Dacomitinib from cif to pdb format:
Download Definition (CIF Format) of 1C9.
Load 1C9.cif into PyMOL
load /path/to/1C9.cif
Convert into a .pdb
save 1C9.pdb
Can be found in PDB, Docking Results, etc. Folder

Procedure to clean pdb 4DXD of S. Aureus by removal of inhibitor (723) to just get FtsZ (happens via Allosteric pocket):
Notes: GDP turns into GTP (energy source) not affected
When inhibitor binds to allosteric site → prevents the filament formations in order to perform the function of bacterial cellular division help

Download 4DXD PDB from https://www.rcsb.org/structure/4DXD via legacy PDB format
Open PyMOL (download link: https://www.pymol.org/)
Goal: To remove inhibitor 723 (PC190723) and remove any other heteroatoms 
Perform next in terminal
load /path/to/4DXD.pdb 
remove hetatm and not resn GDP (removes all heteroatoms such as water including 723 and leaves GDP)

save 4DXD_clean_with_GDP.pdb (locate it into your storage) 
Can be found in PDB, Docking Results, etc. Folder
